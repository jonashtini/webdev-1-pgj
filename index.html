ABOUT


Gjenerali Beach is the most beautiful and attractive part of the entire Kavaja Riviera consisting of 30 km of coastline, which makes you fall in love with it from the moment it suddenly appears on the horizon, just after you have crossed the hills of Kryevidhi. The rocky capes that surround it are conglomeratic and have filled the coast with small colorful objects that form an amazing mosaic that harmonizes with the blue of the water, the green of the bushes and the yellow of the beach sand. The aquatic world is rich with fish of different types and a special species of sea turtle which has chosen this small beach to breed its species.


The panorama of the bay is fantastic, it creates a complete feeling of calmness and relaxation, makes you fall in love with nature. In recent years, a small tourist complex has been carved into the hills of this bay, which first of all has respected nature, using only wooden structures, from small villas, apartments to restaurant verandas that offer seafood cuisine. and fresh fish for many vacationers. Everything is completely in harmony and placed where it should be, adding only beauty and no burden to the interior.

ACCOMODATION


Set in Kavajë, a few steps from General Beach, Plazhi i Gjeneralit offers accommodation with a garden, free private parking, a terrace and a restaurant. Offering a bar, the property is located within 24 km of Rock of Kavaje. The accommodation provides a concierge service, dry cleaning services and currency exchange for guests. At the hotel, all rooms are equipped with a balcony. à la carte and Italian breakfast options are available every morning at Plazhi i Gjeneralit. Durres Amphiteatre is 35 km from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 59 km from Plazhi i Gjeneralit.
